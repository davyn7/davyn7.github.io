My name is Davyn Sudirdjo.

Fun fact: I am not entirely sure of the intended pronunciation of my name in English.

I was born and raised in Jakarta, Indonesia, and am currently a junior at Stanford University pursuing a BA in Economics with minors in Computer Science and Data Science. The three words I would use to describe myself are hungry, passionate, and innovative.

Hungry There are two ways that hunger defines me. On one hand, I am a very pragmatic. My results-driven nature is my drive. I am hungry for change in Indonesia's socioeconomic landscape, and this hunger directed me into entrepreneurship. On the other hand, I love food. I have not tasted a cuisine that I dislike, and I love trying everything from street food to Michelin-starred food. I travel not for the sights, but primarily for the food.
Passionate My passion to boost socioeconomic development in Indonesia stems from an experience I had when I was 12 and my life has been changed ever since. To me, having passion means that I will not give up until there isn't a single ounce of effort left in me. Having passion also means that I will always give more than 100% to see whatever I am working on through.
Innovative I believe that innovation is the only way that Indonesia can see change and catch up technologically with the global superpowers. Innovation is also one of my main reasons on why I chose to go to Stanford - I want to be in the forefront of the technology revolution and create novel solutions to bring my dreams and passion into reality.
