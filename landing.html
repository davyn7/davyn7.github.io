Entrepreneurship

I am a passionate and impact-driven entrepreneur. Here is my story.

It all began with a dream

My journey has been anything but easy, and here are some key highlights of how I got to who I am now. All entrepreneurial highlights can be found on the entrepreneurship section of my ePortfolio!

Dusun Koko (Koko Village)

When I was 12, my parents sent me to a remote village in one of Indonesia’s most rural provinces. I was there as an english and math TA for kindergarten students. I was inspired by their dreams, dreams that knew no financial limitations. I wanted to help improve their quality of lives and living standards, and that was when I found my entrepreneurial drive.

  • Learn more

Socialization with Farmers

eTani is a startup that I founded in 2016 to improve the living standards of Indonesian farmers. I went to a different village every weekend of my senior year of high school to socialise with farmers. This part of the journey was long and winded, but I’m very proud to say that we’ve onboarded 10,000 farmers ever since.

  • Learn more

Pancasila

Pancasila is Indonesia’s founding ideology and is made up of 5 principles. The government saw my work with eTani and I was very humbled to be appointed as the ambassador for the “social justice” principle. I have since then been asked to speak at different universities across Indonesia, and go on national ad campaigns alongside Olympic gold-medalists.
